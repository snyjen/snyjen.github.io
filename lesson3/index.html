Weather Digest

weatherdigest.com

Purpose

  • The main purpose of weatherdigest.com will be to give current weather forecasts for certain areas of Idaho including Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. It will also include a Storm Center and Gallery. It will serve the community and the region as well as travelers who are visiting the area, being able to plan outings and activities according to the weather.
  • The second purpose is to offer information about outdoor activities offered in the areas. With current weather forecasts, residents and visitors will be able to plan activities according to the weather.
  • The weatherdigest.com will also collect information from users to offer weather forecasts for specific areas.
