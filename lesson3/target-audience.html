Target Audience

The target audience of weatherdigest.com are those living in Eastern Idaho in the areas specified as well as visitors to the area who want to enjoy outdoor activities and want to know the weather forecast:

They are adults with families age 25-40 who want to spend the weekend outside and retired couples age 55-70 who enjoy the outdoors and activities. Personas

  • Anna is 32 years old and has a small family, a husband and two young children. She has a degree in Graphic Design and works from home. Anna and her family like to spend the weekends exploring the outdoors and going on walks. She likes to be able to plan for the weather, so she can prepare as necessary. Anna uses her cell phone to check the weather.

  • Barbara is 66 years old and retired with her husband. They like to spend a lot of time outdoors and attending events and activities. Barbara likes to know what the weather will be to make sure they will have jackets if it might be cold and hats and sunscreen if hot and sunny. Barbara likes to check the weather on the computer.

Scenarios

  1. Should we take a hike in the morning to avoid the afternoon heat?
  2. It looks cloudy today, will it rain or is it just an overcast day?
  3. How hot will it be today? Will I need to take extra water bottles?
  4. It has been raining the past few days, will it still be raining today? I would like to take a walk outside.
  5. It is warming up, how hot will it be today? I would like to spend some time at the lake.
