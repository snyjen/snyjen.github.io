Color Scheme

I chose the three colors of blue, white, and yellow as the color scheme for the site. The colors were chosen to compliment and go with the logo. The header is in #2a5aa8, blue. The navigation is in #ffdc73, yellow. And the main body is in #ffffff, white. The main colors are white and blue, with accents of yellow.
