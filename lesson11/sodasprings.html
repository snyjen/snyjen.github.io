Children's Authors Online Read Alouds

by Jennifer Snyder

With parents and children looking for ways to learn and occupy their time, reading is an excellent way to fulfill the days at home. Many childrens authors have taken the initiative to read aloud their books online. This is a great way to deviate from the norm. Along with reading their books, there are also activities to do. The website We Are Teachers has complied a list of authors and links to their stories read aloud. They are further broken into sections depending on school age, elementary, middle, high school, and young adults.

You can find the complete list with links here : The Big List of Children's Authors Doing Online Read Alouds & Activities
