List of Things to Do

by Jennifer Snyder

A list of things to do during the COVID closures is making the rounds on the internet. The principle Kim Jones McClelland shared teacher Wade Whitehead's list of activities to do while away from school. The site We Are Teachers shared the list and added a few of their own suggestions. The list varies in activities and there are links included to some of the activities that will help explain ideas. While the list is aimed at younger children, elementary age, any one would have fun tackling the activities on this list!

The list of things to do can be found here : Things To Do During the COVID Closures
