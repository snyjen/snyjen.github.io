Virtual Field Trips

by Jennifer Snyder

With the children out of school, parents are trying to find different ways to teach their children. With the advanced technology we enjoy today museums, zoos, aquariums, and places around the world are now available to view from our homes. The website Adventures in Familyhood put together a list including links and activities of virtual field trips. This is a great opportunity to learn as well as a family fun activity.
